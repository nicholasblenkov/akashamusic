Biography

Lucus is a writer, producer, multi-instrumentalist, and artist whose sound fuses a wide range of genres into his vision of modern pop.

He began making melodic rap on SoundCloud at 15, quickly gaining traction that brought him to Los Angeles and his first record deal. At 18, he stepped away from music to focus on becoming a fully realised musician and artist.

Now, at 22, and alongside his artistic partner August Grau, Lucus is reintroducing himself with what he considers his true debut: the project Akasha Dirtypop.

A fully realized artist with vision and depth, he represents a rare breed in today's music landscape.
