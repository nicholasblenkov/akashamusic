Biography

Xavier Weeks is a genre fluid artist with a unique sense for visuals, fashion, and aesthetics, giving his work rare creative depth.

With more than 250 million streams, he first broke through in 2019 with Team and later signed with APG/Atlantic. Now, in partnership with Akasha Music, Xavier is entering a new era.

His sound blends late 2000s euro dance pop with trap inspired vocal delivery, creating music that is distinct, intentional, and built for longevity.

His passion for DJ'ing and constant exploration of various subgenres from the 1990s to early 2000s form a core part of his creative identity.
