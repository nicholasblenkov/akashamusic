Biography

August Grau is a lifelong multi-instrumentalist who has spent the last many years perfecting his craft with a focus on creating music with the highest artistic integrity.

Rather than chasing easy placements with loops and beats, he has stayed true to his own artistic vision, developing a rare versatility that sets him apart in today's landscape where true producers are scarce and beatmakers are many.

His range spans from niche sounding TikTok bangers to full fletched pop anthems and completely live, instrument driven rock records, making his skill set both distinctive and in demand.
