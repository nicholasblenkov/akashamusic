Biography

Raw, poetic, and unfiltered. A symbol of wild freedom, raw honesty and quiet strength born from standing alone.

Røverdatter's world lives in contrast between chaos and calm, rebellion and grace.

Born and raised in Denmark, she's been writing songs and playing instruments since the age of five, shaping an instinctive and genuine relationship with music and poetry.
